Ethiopian Police University aims to become a center of excellence in police training and education in Africa by 2030 G.C.

Mission of EPU:

  • Produce police leaders and professionals who i have the required knowledge, skill and attitude by offering quality, relevant and training.
  • Conduct problem solving research and provide consultancy and community service.
  • Provide capacity building assistance to the region police training colleges and centers.
  • Lead the formulation of national standards in police training and education and assure its implementation.

Core Values:

  • Professionalism: to be a competent police officer by applying police principle and ethics respecting the command chain
  • Integrity: to be loyal and protective for institutional, national and international code of conducts in every policing mission or operation
  • Respect for Diversity: to appreciate multiplicity in the institution and communities from different dimensions or contexts
  • Respect for Human Right: to be committed to respect, promote and protect Human Rights within the institution and during any engagement in the community
